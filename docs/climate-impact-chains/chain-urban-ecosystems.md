# Climate Impact Chain 1: Urban Ecosystems Degradation

**Main Risk:** Degradation of urban ecosystems due to urban heat and droughts.

## Key Policy Issues
* Urban heat island and drought degrading green spaces
* Loss of ecosystem services (cooling, recreation, habitat)
* Introduction/expansion of pests & pathogens
* Aquatic stress and species distribution shifts
* Need for resilient urban planning & green infrastructure
* Social vulnerability (elderly, healthâ€‘compromised groups)

## Adaptation Considerations
* Expand and diversify urban green infrastructure
* Improve monitoring (pests, tree health, water quality)
* Integrate heat mitigation in land-use planning
* Early detection & biosecurity protocols for invasive species
* Nature-based solutions for riparian shading & cooling
