## About the Project
VALORADA (Validated Local Risk Actionable Data for Adaptation) is a Horizon Europe project (Grant Agreement No. 101112837) whose goal is to empower European regions and cities to steer societal transformation toward sustainable, climate‚Äëresilient development. It aligns with the EU Adaptation Mission supporting 150 regions to become climate‚Äëresilient by 2030 through:

* Demonstrating systemic adaptation pathways
* Improving access to and usability of climate data
